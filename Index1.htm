Greetings of the Day!!!!

The ongoing pages were created with a point of view to let you know about myself in a more vivid manner.

I am working as Senior Assistant Professor of Hindi in the department of VVBIS & IS (PU)   at Sadhu Ashram, Hoshiarpur, India, a subsidiary research wing of Panjab University at Chandigarh, India which is working in the field of Sanskrit and Indological Studies. Although my subject of teaching is Hindi but I got interest in scientific Indology and thus, got involved in the field of Indology and Sanskrit literature as well and thus shared my view points in the form of research papers in various seminars in India and abroad. Some of the papers have been published under Common Creative License My basic goal is to combine my range of experience with my ability and understanding as a  teacher cum researcher who wants to make a positive contribution to larger community. I would welcome an interaction with you in the forms of suggestion on the various fields of knowledge including medicine and allied fields, including Social and behavioral sciences  in order to improve my view point.

 

I hope to hear from you at your earliest convenience.
